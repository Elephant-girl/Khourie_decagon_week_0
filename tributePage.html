Frida Khalo (1907-1954)

Frida Kahlo was a Mexican painter who is considered to be one of the greatest painters of all time. Known for her breathtaking portraits, the woman once regarded as "just the wife of Diego Rivera (another painter), she worked to prove herself an equally competent artist with her works displayed in galleries all over the world. She is credited with over 200 paintings. In the aftermath of her death i 1954, her work has been commemorated in several ways including a museum and gallery.
